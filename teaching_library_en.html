Welcome to your own teaching library

apokatalasso provides audio, video and written resources - fruit of over 25 years of service and walking with God. Believers, bible school students, ministers and leaders from different cultures have equally benefited from these teachings and trainings.

These teachings come out of a lifetime of exegesis and research into biblical texts, and seeking what the Spirit is saying today about God's eternity to us and to the church. So that we may tangibly demonstrate Christ's reign on earth in the sphere of each one.
It is said of Erwin that "He is a teacher who plunges us into the depths of what the Bible teaches about God and the human being. He challenges every believer to apply an "ontological" theology and way of life."

We thank you for your interest and believe that these resources will bring new insights, a renewed understanding and matured growth as they have for so many others.
